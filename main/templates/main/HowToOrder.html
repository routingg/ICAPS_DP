How to Order Jamu

Follow these simple steps to use the Mendjamoo dispenser.


Step 1 – Know what you want?

If not, scan the QR code for your personalized Jamu recommendation.

Step 2 – Grab a cup and place it on the tray

Where your Jamu will be poured.

Step 3 – Choose your Jamu

press the left or right button to scroll through options, then press the middle button to start.

Step 4 – Wait a moment

your Jamu will be ready soon!
