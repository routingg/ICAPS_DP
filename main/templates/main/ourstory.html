Our Story

Hello, we are Team.16 from the iCaps International Capstone Program.
We first met in Hanoi, Vietnam during DCamp (Feb 9–15), where we formed a cross-cultural team of passionate students from Korea and Indonesia.
Together, we explored the intersection of tradition and technology through our joint project on Jamu — Indonesia’s traditional herbal wellness drink — enhanced with AI-powered personalization.
From late-night brainstorming to Zoom meetings across borders, we stayed committed to building a meaningful solution — and stronger friendships along the way.
