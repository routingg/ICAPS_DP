Indonesian Traditional Medicine: Jamu

Jamu culture represents precious family heritage passed down from grandmother to mother, and mother to daughter.

Jamu, the general term for traditional herbal drinks that help improve digestion and maintain overall health, is a daily way of life and a cornerstone of culture across the Indonesian archipelago. “Jamu is all about accessible wellness, and that’s what makes it so powerful; it’s not just a beverage, it’s the concept of taking care of yourself,” says Metta Murdaya, founder of jamu-based beauty line Juara and author of Jamu Lifestyle: Indonesian Herbal Wellness Tradition. A typical jamu juice, which falls somewhere on the bitter and sweet spectrum in taste, is created first by blending together a combination of roots, herbs, and spices to balance taste and desired health benefits. Then, the mixture is boiled in water until it’s ready to be bottled and served at room temperature later.

Nature's Pharmacy: Jamu Components

Turmeric (Kunyit)

The golden rhizome that serves as Jamu's core ingredient with excellent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant.

Ginger (Jahe)

A warming spice effective for cold relief, digestive stimulation, and body temperature regulation.

Galangal (Kencur)

Traditional Indonesian herb that shows excellent effectiveness in relieving cough and body aches.

Tamarind (Asem)

Tropical fruit that provides digestive improvement and refreshing sourness, balancing the Jamu flavors.

Temulawak

A type of Javanese traditional turmeric that supports liver health and immunity enhancement.

Palm Sugar (Gula Merah)

Natural sweetener and mineral source that adds authentic sweetness to Jamu preparations.

Lime (Jeruk Nipis)

Rich in vitamin C and freshness, completing the taste and nutritional profile of Jamu drinks.

Lemongrass (Serai)

Herb that provides subtle aroma and digestive benefits, giving Jamu a clean and refreshing aftertaste.

Cinnamon (Kayu Manis)

Offers blood sugar regulation and antioxidant effects while adding warm, sweet fragrance to Jamu.

Cardamom (Kapulaga)

Effective spice for digestive stimulation and breath freshening, providing premium aroma to Jamu blends.

Cloves (Cengkeh)

Aromatic flower buds with powerful antibacterial properties, excellent for oral health and digestive stimulation.

Sappan Wood (Kayu Secang)

Traditional red wood that enhances blood circulation and provides rich antioxidants with distinctive red coloring.

Black Cumin (Jinten Hitam)

Powerful immunity booster known for respiratory health benefits and comprehensive wellness support.

Black Turmeric (Temu Ireng)

Rare and precious ingredient for blood purification, cholesterol reduction, and deep detoxification processes.

Bay Leaves (Daun Salam)

Indonesian aromatic leaves that support blood sugar control and enhance digestive health naturally.

Beras Kencur is an energizing Indonesian herbal drink made from rice, galangal, ginger, and turmeric that provides natural energy boost and relieves fatigue, cough, and body aches with its sweet, refreshing taste and mild spicy kick.

Digestive Health Menstrual Relief Detox

Kunyit Asem is a digestive harmony drink featuring turmeric, tamarind, and palm sugar that improves digestion, regulates menstruation, and relieves stomach issues with its perfectly balanced tangy-sweet flavor enhanced by subtle turmeric aroma.

Temulawak

Liver Health Immunity Boost Relaxation

Temulawak is a relaxation and recovery drink made from Javanese turmeric, regular turmeric, and ginger that promotes stress relief, better sleep, and immunity boost with its complex bitter-sweet flavor that embodies nature's healing power.

Empon-Empon

Complete Immunity Antioxidant Rich Overall Wellness

Empon-Empon is the ultimate comprehensive herbal blend featuring temulawak, turmeric, red ginger, lemongrass, cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon that provides maximum immunity support and overall health benefits with its complex, warming flavor profile.
