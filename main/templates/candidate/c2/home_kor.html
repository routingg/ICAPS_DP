Indonesian Traditional Medicine: Jamu

Jamu culture represents precious family heritage passed down from grandmother to mother, and mother to daughter. Each household possesses unique recipes that express not just medical knowledge, but love and care.

Jamu is a form of herbal medicine and treatment that has been practiced in Indonesia since the eighth century, inscribed in 2023 on the Representative List of the Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity by UNESCO. It is based on the belief of curing hot diseases with medicines of a cold nature, and cold diseases with medicines of a hot nature, with a healthy condition being a balance between hot and cold elements in the body. The colors and shapes of plants are associated with the colors and shapes of the organs whose health they promote. Jamu aims to increase immunity and maintain health. Bearers and practitioners include Jamu makers and the people who make, distribute, cultivate and consume the ingredients. People of all ages take Jamu medicine, and though anyone can make Jamu, most Jamu makers are adult women. The medicine is prepared using herbs and spices often planted by Jamu makers themselves, who then concoct recipes tailored to each customer's age, lifestyle, and health concerns. The practice is transmitted informally, typically within families and among neighbors.

Nature's Pharmacy: Jamu Components

Turmeric (Kunyit)

The golden rhizome that serves as Jamu's core ingredient with excellent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant.

Ginger (Jahe)

A warming spice effective for cold relief, digestive stimulation, and body temperature regulation.

Galangal (Kencur)

Traditional Indonesian herb that shows excellent effectiveness in relieving cough and body aches.

Tamarind (Asem)

Tropical fruit that provides digestive improvement and refreshing sourness, balancing the Jamu flavors.

Temulawak

A type of Javanese traditional turmeric that supports liver health and immunity enhancement.

Palm Sugar (Gula Merah)

Natural sweetener and mineral source that adds authentic sweetness to Jamu preparations.

Lime (Jeruk Nipis)

Rich in vitamin C and freshness, completing the taste and nutritional profile of Jamu drinks.

Lemongrass (Serai)

Herb that provides subtle aroma and digestive benefits, giving Jamu a clean and refreshing aftertaste.

Cinnamon (Kayu Manis)

Offers blood sugar regulation and antioxidant effects while adding warm, sweet fragrance to Jamu.

Cardamom (Kapulaga)

Effective spice for digestive stimulation and breath freshening, providing premium aroma to Jamu blends.

Discover the balance of body and mind through nature's healing power

Beras Kencur

Beras Kencur is an energizing Indonesian herbal drink made from rice, galangal, ginger, and turmeric that provides natural energy boost and relieves fatigue, cough, and body aches with its sweet, refreshing taste and mild spicy kick.

Kunyit Asem

Kunyit Asem is a digestive harmony drink featuring turmeric, tamarind, and palm sugar that improves digestion, regulates menstruation, and relieves stomach issues with its perfectly balanced tangy-sweet flavor enhanced by subtle turmeric aroma.

Temulawak

Temulawak is a relaxation and recovery drink made from Javanese turmeric, regular turmeric, and ginger that promotes stress relief, better sleep, and immunity boost with its complex bitter-sweet flavor that embodies nature's healing power.
